HTML ENTITIES

An HTML entity is a piece of text ("string") that begins with an ampersand (&) and ends with a semi-colon (:). Entities are frequently used to display reserved characters (which would otherwise be interpreted as HTML code), and invisibke characters (like non-breaking spaces). You can also use them in place of other characters that are difficult to type with a standard keyboard.
